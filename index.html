About Square Dance

Square Dance is a challenging, high-energy activity for people of all ages. This 300-year-old folk tradition is also the official dance of nineteen US states.

Heel Cloppers

Heel Cloppers is a square dance club that danced for 50 years in the Tulsa, Oklahoma area. In 2011, we shut our doors; this site lives on as a resource for square dancers in Green Country, Northeast Oklahoma.
